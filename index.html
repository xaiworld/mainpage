Q&A

Things people ask me.

How did you get into playing brass instruments?

I picked up the trumpet in school and never looked back. There's something about the resonance of brass that just speaks to me. Over the years I've expanded to other instruments in the family.

What do you grow in your garden?

A mix of vegetables, herbs, and native flowers. I'm especially into tomatoes, basil, and pollinator-friendly plants. The garden changes every season — check gardening.xai.world for updates.

Bagpipes — really?

Really! The Great Highland Bagpipe is an incredible instrument once you get past the learning curve. I play with a local pipe band and occasionally at events. It's loud, it's proud, and it's a lot of fun.

What tech stack do you use for these sites?

This main page is plain HTML and CSS hosted on Render. The subdomains vary — some are static, some use lightweight frameworks. I believe in keeping things simple until complexity is actually needed.

Can I reach out to you?

Sure! The best way is through my Instagram DMs or by opening an issue on the GitHub repo.
